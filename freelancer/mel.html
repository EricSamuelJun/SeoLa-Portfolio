Hello.

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Management at the Yale School of Management. I will be joining the USC Marshall School of Business as an Assistant Professor of Management & Organization in the summer of 2022.

I study entrepreneurship, human capital, labor markets, and resource mobilization in innovation-driven environments. Throughout my research, I focus on how the backgrounds and social dynamics of individuals––such as entrepreneurs, investors, and innovators––affect performance and innovation.

My research was recognized with the 2020 Best Entrepreneurship Paper Award from the Organization and Management Theory (OMT) division of the Academy of Management and the 2020 Kauffman Knowledge Challenge Grant.

I earned my B.A. in Economics at the University of Pennsylvania and M.A.& M.Phil. in Management from Yale University. Prior to my graduate studies, I worked in investment banking at Goldman Sachs. I advised startups in accessing funding as well as strategic and financial investors searching for acquisition targets in the emerging sectors.
