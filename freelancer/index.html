Hello,

I am a Ph.D. student in Carlson School of Management, University of Minnesota.

I study organizational behavior (OB) and human resources management (HRM).

My research interests are largely based upon interpersonal relationship, employee training, socialization, and social class.

I earned my B.A. and B.B.A. in English & English Literature, and Management, and M.S. in Management at the Korea University.

Prior to my graduate studies, I worked in Toyota Motor.

I managed and trained the employees of the Toyota’s nationwide official dealership network in South Korea.
